🧩 Word puzzles

Letters start as puzzle pieces. The child presses each letter on the keyboard, and the matching piece flies into place.

🎵 Sounds and movement

Every key has its own generated sound and its own motion style: slide, pop, jump, wiggle, spin, or float. The chosen theme adds extra rockets, aliens, pets, comets, moon dust, signals, or constellations.

📘 Workbook activities

Sequence missions now show one short workbook line at a time, including space. When the child finishes the line, the next example refreshes onto the screen. Word lists can still be expanded later.
